Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Description

Discover how to make superb spaghetti carbonara. This cheesy pasta dish is an Italian favourite and with the right technique, you can make it perfect every time

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Water
  • Bacon
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Fry the bacon strips. Try not to eat too much of it
  2. Boil pasta in Water, add some salt to this
  3. Mix cheese, milk and cook white pasta sauce
  4. Put in pasta and cook for some time
  5. Add salt to taste lol
