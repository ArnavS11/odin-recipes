Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Description

Quick, Easy and Delicious-Homemade pizza crust and tomato sauce has never been easier-and this recipe for pepperoni pizza produces a delicious classic!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Pizza sauce
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Spread pizza sauce on dough
  2. Add cheese
  3. Add pepperoni
  4. Bake until it is done
  5. Add salt to taste lol
